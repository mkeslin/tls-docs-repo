# Annual operating plan — 2026 H2

**Period:** July–December 2026\
**Theme:** Build the Platform (see [strategic roadmap](strategic-roadmap-2026-2029.md))

## Mission

Complete Thin Line Platform and prove it can be repeatedly sold, implemented, and supported.

By the end of 2026, Thin Line will have proven that a unified RMS, CAD, Court, and Jail platform can be repeatedly sold, implemented, and supported. We will enter 2027 with a repeatable sales process, strong regional references, and the operational foundation to double again.

***

## Top 10 company priorities

1. Complete Court commercial readiness.
2. Complete Jail commercial readiness.
3. Close Levelland PD.

5. Create the implementation playbook.
6. Automate deployments and data conversion.

## Biggest risks

* Product scope expands faster than revenue.
* Founder becomes implementation bottleneck.
* Large opportunities delay completion of core platform.
* Too much custom development.
* Sales pipeline depends too heavily on a few opportunities.

## Biggest constraint

Complete the platform without sacrificing repeatability.

**Guiding question:** Does this make Thin Line Platform easier to sell, easier to implement, or easier to support? If the answer is no, it should probably wait until 2027.

## Biggest opportunity

Turn satisfied customers into the primary growth engine.

## Things we will not do

* Build highly custom features for individual customers unless they strengthen the core platform.
* Expand outside Texas.
* Chase enterprise-sized agencies that distract from our ICP.
* Hire ahead of proven need.
* Delay Court or Jail completion for low-value requests.
