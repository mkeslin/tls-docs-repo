# Vision and principles

## Vision

Build the leading unified public safety platform for small and mid-sized Texas agencies by replacing disconnected systems with a single, modern platform spanning law enforcement, dispatch, courts, corrections, and public safety administration.

## Product

**Thin Line Platform** — a unified platform spanning RMS, CAD, mobile workflows, courts, jails/corrections, and related administrative functions.

## Core principles

1. **Build once. Reuse everywhere.**
2. **Standardize before customizing.**
3. **Regional dominance before national expansion.**
4. **Product decisions drive long-term value.**
5. **Customer success creates future sales.**
6. **Automate before hiring.**
7. **Simplicity scales.**
