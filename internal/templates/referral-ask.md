# Referral ask script

**Document type:** Template  
**Status:** v0.1  
**Audience:** Internal  
**Related:** [Capture referral](../sops/advocate/capture-referral.md)

Use natural language. This is a prompt, not a teleprompter.

---

## When

After a clear success moment (go-live going well, strong check-in, unsolicited praise). Skip if the relationship is strained.

---

## Ask (example)

> We’ve been glad to partner with [Agency]. As we grow carefully in West Texas, peer introductions mean a lot.  
> Are there any chiefs or agencies you’d be comfortable introducing us to — especially nearby departments that might be evaluating RMS, CAD, court, or jail systems?  
> If you’re willing, may we use your name when we reach out?

---

## Capture checklist

- [ ] Agency name(s)  
- [ ] Person / role if known  
- [ ] Context (why they might care)  
- [ ] Permission to use referrer’s name — Yes / No  
- [ ] Logged in Pipedrive (referrer + referred)  
- [ ] Next activity scheduled for first touch  

---

## If they want time

> Totally fine — I’ll check back [date]. No pressure either way.

Schedule the follow-up ask in Pipedrive.
