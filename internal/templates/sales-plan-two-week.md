## Example (illustrative)

*Example only — replace dates, agency names, and targets for the real period. Numbers are starting guidance, not policy.*

### Eric Fugate — sales plan (2 weeks)

**Period:** Mon Jul 27 – Fri Aug 7, 2026  
**Focus:** Priority 1 (Lubbock / West Texas) · LE ICP · CopSync signal when known  
**System of record:** Pipedrive  

#### Goals (end of 2 weeks)

| Goal | Target |
|------|--------|
| New LE conversations started (reply, call connect, or in-person) | **8** |
| Chiefs-list emails sent (ICP-filtered) | **20** |
| Referral asks completed + logged | **3** |
| Field / territory half-day or full day | **1** |
| Pipedrive: every touched org has origin + next activity | **100%** |

#### Week 1 — List + warm follow-up

| Day | Block | Work |
|-----|-------|------|
| Mon | 90 min | Pull 20 Priority-1 LE agencies from South Plains chiefs list (ICP filter). Create/update Pipedrive orgs. Origin = Association list. |
| Mon–Tue | 2× 60 min | Send first-touch emails (batches of 10). Log activity; set follow-up task +3 business days. |
| Wed | 60 min | Pipeline review: open activities, stalled deals, named late-stage opportunities. |
| Thu | 90 min | Call non-responders from Mon batch (or leave VM + note). |
| Fri | 45 min | **Referral asks:** 3 healthy customers / recent wins — ask + log. |
| Fri | 30 min | Build **field-day stop list** for Week 2 (6–10 agencies near hub). |

#### Week 2 — Field day + close the loop

| Day | Block | Work |
|-----|-------|------|
| Mon | AM | Confirm field-day route; pre-load Pipedrive stops. |
| Tue | **Full day** | Territory day (example hub: agencies near Lubbock). Walk-ins / brief intros. Same-day log: Origin = Field visit; next activity on every stop. |
| Wed | 60 min | Follow-up emails/calls from field day. |
| Thu | 60 min | Second touch on Week 1 email batch still silent but worth pursuing. |
| Fri | 45 min | Scorecard + next 2-week draft (what worked / what to repeat). |

#### Named priorities (keep moving in parallel)

- Active close / late-stage: *[fill real names — e.g. Levelland PD, Winkler, Jarrell]*  
- CopSync displacement leads: *[list 2–3 if known]*  
- Do **not** spend cold outbound time on standalone court or multi-clerk / multi-judge courts  

#### Definition of done (Friday check-in)

Bring Pipedrive showing:

1. Count of emails sent + conversations started  
2. Three referral ask notes  
3. Field-day stops logged with next activities  
4. Next week’s first outbound block already on the calendar
