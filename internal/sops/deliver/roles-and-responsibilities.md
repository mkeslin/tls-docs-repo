# Roles and responsibilities

**Phase:** Deliver  
**Document type:** Reference  
**Status:** v1  

Who owns what across the Deliver lifecycle. Adjust names per engagement; keep the **roles**.

---

## Core roles

| Role | Primary accountability |
|------|------------------------|
| **Sales owner** | Commercial clarity, handoff packet, scope flags at contract |
| **Implementation lead** | Lifecycle board, phase exit criteria, customer coordination |
| **Migration specialist** | Legacy assessment, packages, Overrides, validation (when migration in scope) |
| **Infrastructure operator** | Bootstrap, environment health, deploy of VersionBranch |
| **Configuration specialist** | Agency & Module Settings, officers/users/codes |
| **Integrations / hardware owner** | Third-party interfaces and device readiness |
| **Trainer** | Training plan and delivery |
| **Customer sponsor** | Agency decisions, validation sign-off, go-live authority |
| **Support (Operate)** | Receives hypercare handoff; steady-state tickets |

One person may hold multiple roles on a small engagement. Record owners at Kickoff.

---

## RACI by phase (summary)

| Phase | Accountable (A) | Responsible (R) | Consulted (C) |
|-------|-----------------|-----------------|---------------|
| 0 Sales handoff | Sales owner | Sales owner | Implementation lead |
| 1 Kickoff and discovery | Implementation lead | Implementation lead | Sales, Customer sponsor |
| 2 Infrastructure | Implementation lead | Infrastructure operator | Engineering (platform) |
| 3 Data migration | Implementation lead | Migration specialist | Customer sponsor, Engineering (packages) |
| 4 Configuration | Implementation lead | Configuration specialist | Customer sponsor |
| 5 Integrations and hardware | Implementation lead | Integrations / hardware owner | Customer IT, vendors |
| 6 Training | Implementation lead | Trainer | Customer sponsor |
| 7 Go live | Implementation lead | Implementation lead | Customer sponsor, Support |
| 8 Hypercare and transition | Implementation lead → Support | Implementation lead / Support | Customer sponsor |

**A** = single owner of the outcome · **R** = does the work · **C** = must be heard before exit.

---

## Customer obligations (typical)

- Provide contacts, decisions, and access on agreed dates  
- Complete validation / acceptance for migration when in scope  
- Designate trainees and a go-live sponsor  
- Supply integration credentials and hardware (or approve Thin Line quotes)
