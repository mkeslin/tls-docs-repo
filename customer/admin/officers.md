# Officers

Maintain the officer roster used on citations, incidents, and related records.

## Open Officers

1. Open **Admin**.
2. Choose **Officers**.

Requires the officer-administration claim.

## What to maintain

| Field / area | Why it matters |
|--------------|----------------|
| **Badge / ID** | Appears on tickets and reports |
| **Name and dates** | Employment / certification windows your agency tracks |
| **Image** | Officer photo when your agency uses it |
| **Active** | Inactive officers should not be selected for new work |

Officers are often created as part of **Add User/Officer** under [Users, roles, and claims](users-roles-claims.md). Use **Officers** to keep badge and profile data current without changing login claims.

## Typical workflow

1. Search or open the officer.
2. Edit badge, dates, image, or active status.
3. Save.
4. Spot-check a citation or incident picker to confirm the officer appears correctly.

## Tips

- Align badge numbers with your agency’s paper / MDT conventions before go-live.
- Deactivate separated officers promptly; do not reuse badges without a clear agency policy.
- Users who are not officers still need accounts for clerk / records / jail roles — officer profile is optional for them.
