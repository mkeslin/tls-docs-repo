# Audit, security, and jobs

Optional Admin screens for compliance review and operations. Most agencies use these less often than Users, Codes, or Agency settings.

## Audit History

**Admin → Audit History** (requires audit access).

Search the RMS audit trail for who changed what and when. Use for investigations, QA, or answering “who updated this record?”

## Security Log

**Admin → Security Log** (same audit-access claim family).

Search security-related events (sign-in / access patterns your build records). Use with your agency’s CJIS and internal security procedures.

## Scheduler & Jobs

**Admin → Scheduler & Jobs** (requires scheduler admin).

View or administer background jobs and history. Typical day-to-day clerks do not need this screen. Escalate recurring job failures to Thin Line Support with the job name and time.

## CAD Admin (when enabled)

**Admin → CAD** appears when CAD is enabled for your agency and you hold CAD admin rights.

Use it for CAD agency / unit administration your deployment expects. Full CAD operator training lives under [CAD](../cad/README.md) (guide expanding).

## Evidence Admin

An Evidence Admin route may exist as a **placeholder** (“utilities coming soon”). Property-room work uses the [Evidence](../rms/evidence/README.md) module and incident property flows — not this Admin screen.
