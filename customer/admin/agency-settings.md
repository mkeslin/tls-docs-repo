# Agency settings

Configure agency identity, notifications, and print headers under **Agency & Module Settings**.

## Open Agency settings

1. Open **Admin**.
2. Choose **Agency & Module Settings**.

Requires the agency-administration claim.

## Tabs

| Tab | What agencies typically change |
|-----|--------------------------------|
| **General** | Agency display name and related identity; court / import options your claim allows |
| **Modules** | View which modules are enabled and numbering patterns — **usually view-only**; contact Thin Line Support to change enablement |
| **Notifications & Workflow** | Dashboard / task-board notification preferences |
| **Reports** | Names and addresses that appear on report output |
| **Documents** | Header information for printed documents |

Some fields on **General** are maintained by Thin Line Support only. If a control is disabled, request the change through Support rather than inventing a workaround.

## Typical workflows

### Update report / document headers

1. Open **Reports** or **Documents**.
2. Edit agency name, address, or other header fields shown.
3. Save.
4. Run a sample report or print from a training record to verify layout.

### Adjust notifications

1. Open **Notifications & Workflow**.
2. Enable or disable dashboard / task notifications that match your staffing.
3. Save and confirm with a user who should receive those notices.

### Review modules

1. Open **Modules**.
2. Confirm purchased products appear as expected.
3. If a module is missing or numbering is wrong, open a [support request](../support/support-request.md) — do not expect self-service toggles for every setting.

## Tips

- Keep printed headers aligned with letterhead and court stationery.
- After changing General court/import options, validate with a citation → court path in a training tenant first.
- Multi-agency or record-sharing options, when present, are Thin Line Support territory for most customers.
