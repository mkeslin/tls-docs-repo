# Payments and remittance

Record and review collections payments.

## Payment History

1. Open **Collections** → **Payment History**.
2. Search by account, date, or other criteria shown.
3. Open a row to review the payment detail.

Use this for audit and “what was posted” questions.

## Payment Entry

Requires collections **modify** rights.

1. Open **Collections** → **Payment Entry**.
2. Identify the referred account.
3. Enter remittance amount, date, and method/reference as shown.
4. Save / post per the screen actions.

## Payment Import (CSV)

Requires collections **modify** rights.

1. Open **Collections** → **Payment Import**.
2. Follow the import template your agency and Thin Line agreed with the vendor.
3. Upload the CSV and resolve any validation errors before confirming.
4. Spot-check **Payment History** after import.

## Tips

- Prefer the vendor file format Thin Line configured for your agency — ad-hoc columns fail validation.
- Do not double-enter the same remittance in Payment Entry and Import.
