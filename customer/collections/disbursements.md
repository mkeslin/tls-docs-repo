# Disbursements

Report and batch disbursement activity for collections.

## Disbursement Report

1. Open **Collections** → **Disbursement Report**.
2. Set the report period and filters.
3. Run / print / export as your agency process requires.

## Disbursement Batches

Requires collections **modify** rights for batch maintenance.

1. Open **Collections** → **Disbursement Batches**.
2. Search existing batches or create a new batch per your close-out process.
3. Open a batch to review included activity and complete batch actions shown on the screen.

## Tips

- Align batch dates with your finance / city treasurer calendar.
- Escalate posting mismatches to your court administrator and Thin Line Support with batch id and dates.
