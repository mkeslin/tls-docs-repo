# Collections

Customer guides for the **Collections (COL)** module — referred court accounts, remittance entry/import, and disbursement reporting.

## What Collections is

When eligible court cases are **referred** to collections (for example under your state’s collections statute / Art. 103.0031-style process), the **Collections** module is where staff work the referred portfolio, record payments from the collections vendor, and run disbursement reports and batches.

Case-level court payments still start in [Court — Payments](../court/payments.md). Accounting close-out may continue in [Accounting](../accounting/README.md).

## Who this guide is for

- Court / collections clerks who maintain referred accounts
- Staff who enter or import vendor remittances
- Supervisors who run disbursement reports and batches
