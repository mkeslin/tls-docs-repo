# Jail

Customer guides for **Jail Intake** and the **Jail Command Center (JLM)** — Thin Line’s corrections workflows from booking through housing, observation, medication, release, and transfer.

## What Jail is

Jail in Thin Line has two closely linked surfaces:

| Surface | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **Jail Intake** | Create and complete **bookings** (person, custody, charges, property, medical/mental health, classification) until the person is accepted into custody |
| **Jail Command Center (JLM / JCC)** | Live operations board for people **in custody** — roster/layout, housing moves, cell checks, work queue, release, and offsite transfer |

A **booking** is the intake unit of work. When staff **Accept Into Custody**, a **custody episode** appears on the Command Center for day-to-day floor operations.

## Who this guide is for

- Jail intake / booking officers
- Floor officers and supervisors using the Command Center
- Jail administrators who configure locations and review reports
- Agency staff who search bookings, passes, or medication logs
