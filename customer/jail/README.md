# Jail

Customer guides for **Jail Intake** and the **Jail Command Center** — booking through housing, observation, medication, release, and transfer.

## What Jail is

| Surface | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **Jail Intake** | Create and complete **bookings** until **Accept into Custody** |
| **Jail Command Center** (header **JAIL**) | Live board for people **in custody** — roster/layout, moves, passes, meds, release, offsite transfer |

A **booking** is the intake unit of work. After accept, a **custody episode** appears on the Command Center.
