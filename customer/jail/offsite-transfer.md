# Offsite transfer

![Jail Command Center](images/jail-command-center.png)

Move a person to an offsite holding arrangement with a transfer packet.

## When to use

Use **Transfer out** / the **Offsite Transfer** wizard when policy requires a documented packet (not only a casual [housing move](housing-and-moves.md) to an offsite location type).

## Wizard

1. Open the episode → **Transfer out** (dialog **Offsite Transfer**).
2. **Offsite Details & Location**
   - **Offsite holding location** (required)
   - **Offsite / receiving facility name** (required)
   - Expected return, billing note, receiving agency / ORI / identifier, notes as applicable
   - **Next**
3. **Packet Checklist & Forms** — complete checklist items; generate **Texas uniform health PDF** / **Mental health PDF** when your process uses them.
4. **Complete transfer packet**.

Canceling mid-wizard may undo a provisional move — confirm board state after cancel.

## After transfer

- Person appears under **Offsite** on the Command Center when the transfer completed.
- Retain packet PDFs per facility records rules ([Reports](reports.md)).
