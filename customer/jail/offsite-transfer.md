# Offsite transfer

Moving a person out of the facility on an offsite / transfer-out path.

## When to use transfer vs release

| Path | Typical use |
|------|-------------|
| **Release** | Custody ends; person leaves your jail’s custody |
| **Offsite transfer** | Person remains in a custody/transfer workflow but leaves the facility (or moves to offsite holding) per your agency process |

Use the path your facility trained — do not use release to simulate a transfer packet.

## Transfer-out wizard

From the Command Center episode actions:

1. Start **offsite transfer** / transfer-out (wording may vary).
2. Complete the wizard checklist and destination / context fields.
3. Generate or attach required documents when prompted (including health transfer paperwork when configured for Texas Uniform Health style packets).
4. Submit and confirm the episode shows as **offsite** (or equivalent) and leaves onsite housing counts.

## After transfer

- Onsite filters should no longer show the person in facility housing.
- Transfer log / reports can document the movement window — see [Reports and printouts](reports.md).
- Return-to-facility procedures follow your agency policy and available move actions when the person comes back.

## Tips

- Complete medical and property checklist items before transfer when the wizard requires them.
- Print the transfer packet at the time of movement; do not rely on memory for receiving agency paperwork.
- If visitation or court moves are different typed moves, use those move prompts instead of a full offsite transfer.
