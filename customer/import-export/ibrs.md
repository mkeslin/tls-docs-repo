# IBRS

Generate IBRS / NIBRS transmission work from Import/Export.

## Steps

1. Open **Import/Export** from the left rail.
2. Choose **IBRS** (requires IBRS / NIBRS report access).
3. Set the submission period and options shown on the screen.
4. Generate / transmit per your agency’s state process.
5. Keep confirmation artifacts your state requires (download, email, or portal upload).

## Tips

- Fix incident data quality **before** transmission — see [Incidents — Print, attachments, history, and IBRS](../rms/incidents/print-attachments-history-ibrs.md).
- Coordinate who is allowed to submit so two people do not double-send overlapping periods.
