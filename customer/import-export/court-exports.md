# Court exports

Court statutory packs available from the Import/Export menu when you have court access.

| Menu item | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| **DPS Conviction Report** | Conviction reporting pack for DPS-style submission |
| **OCA Report** | Office of Court Administration style reporting |
| **State Quarterly Report** | Quarterly court statistical pack |

## Typical steps

1. Open **Import/Export**.
2. Choose the court export.
3. Set the period and parameters.
4. Generate and submit per your court’s calendar and state instructions.

These packs also appear in the broader court reporting story — see [Court — Reports](../court/reports.md). Prefer one agreed path (Import/Export vs Court Reports) so staff do not produce conflicting files.
