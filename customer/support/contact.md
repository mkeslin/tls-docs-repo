# Contact Thin Line

How to reach Thin Line Software for product support.

## Office hours

**8:00 AM – 5:00 PM Central Time, Monday–Friday**

For after-hours emergencies, leave a message. Thin Line will return the call as soon as practical.

## Phone

**806-300-0455**

## Email

| Purpose | Address |
|---------|---------|
| General | info@thinlinesoftware.com |
| Support / customer success contacts | Your Thin Line implementation or support contact may also share direct addresses during onboarding |

If you already work with a named Thin Line contact from your project, you may continue to use that channel for non-urgent questions.

## What Thin Line vs your agency handles

| Issue | Start with |
|-------|------------|
| Forgotten password, locked account, wrong username | Your **agency administrator** |
| Missing module or dropdown codes for your agency | Your **agency administrator** |
| Product defects, unclear product behavior, outages | **Thin Line** (after agency checks above) |
| Training “how do I…” after go-live | Thin Line and/or your agency trainer — see [Training](../training/README.md) |
