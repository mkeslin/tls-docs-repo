# Troubleshooting

Quick checks before escalating to Thin Line.

## “Username or password is incorrect”

1. Confirm **Caps Lock** is off. Use **Show password** on the login screen if available.
2. Confirm you are using the correct **username** (ask your agency administrator if unsure).
3. Ask your administrator whether the account is **locked or disabled**.
4. Have your administrator issue a **temporary password**, then follow [first-time login](../getting-started/logging-in.md#first-time-login).

## Login succeeds but the app errors or spins

1. Check your computer **clock**. An incorrect system time can block login/session security. Fix the clock, then try again.
2. Confirm your bookmark is **not** only the bare login URL. Prefer a normal app entry such as your agency’s `/dashboard` (or the URL Thin Line gave you at go-live).

## Wrong data or missing modules

1. Confirm the **agency** in the header.
2. Ask your administrator about **permissions** and whether the module is licensed for your agency.
3. See [FAQ](faq.md).

## Still stuck?

Gather the items in [What to include in a support request](support-request.md) and [contact Thin Line](contact.md).
