# FAQ

Common questions. If something here is wrong for your agency, ask your administrator — many answers depend on local permissions and codes.

## Can I add a user if I do not know their final email yet?

**Yes.** You can enter a temporary or personal email and change it later in [Users, roles, and claims](../admin/users-roles-claims.md) when the permanent address is known. Ask your agency administrator if you do not have Admin access.

## Can I retrieve a report that was run in the past?

**Yes.** Reports are retained for a period of time (commonly up to three years). From **Dashboard**, open **Report History**. Users with elevated report claims (or supervisor roles your agency assigns) can see other users’ reports as well.

## Why am I not seeing a module that others see?

- Your **security claims** and agency settings control visible modules. Ask your administrator to check permissions.
- Confirm you are in the **correct agency** (header name/logo). Switch agency from the user avatar menu if needed.

## Why don’t some dropdowns have the values I need?

Most agency-configurable codes (non–NIBRS-fixed lists) are maintained under [Admin — Codes](../admin/codes.md). Request new or updated codes through your agency’s process. Descriptions should be ALL UPPERCASE.
