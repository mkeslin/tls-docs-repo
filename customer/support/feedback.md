# In-app Feedback

Send product comments to Thin Line from inside the application.

## Where to find it

In the RMS shell, open **Feedback** from the lower part of the **right master records rail** (see [Master records](../getting-started/master-records.md)).

## When to use Feedback vs a support call

| Use Feedback for | Prefer phone/email support for |
|------------------|--------------------------------|
| Suggestions and usability comments | You cannot log in |
| Non-urgent product ideas | Production is down or blocked |
| “This screen is confusing” notes | Password / account lockouts |

Feedback is not a substitute for emergency or access issues — use [Contact Thin Line](contact.md).
