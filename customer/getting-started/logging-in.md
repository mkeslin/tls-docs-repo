# Logging in

First-time and everyday access to Thin Line, passwords, and signing out.

## What you need

Thin Line (or your agency administrator) provides:

- Your agency’s **website address** (URL)
- Your **username**
- A **temporary password** (first login or after a reset)

## First-time login

1. Open the website address in a supported browser.
2. Enter your **username** and **temporary password**, then log in.
3. When prompted to change your password:
   - Enter the temporary password as **Current password**
   - Enter and confirm your new password
4. Continue into the application.

### Password requirements

Requirements depend on your agency’s IT / identity setup. At a minimum, use **at least 8 characters**. Prefer a unique password you do not reuse elsewhere.

## Everyday login

1. Open your agency’s Thin Line URL (bookmark `/dashboard` or the landing page Thin Line gave you — not only a stale login-only link).
2. Enter username and password.
3. Select **Log In**.

Confirm the **agency** name in the header after login if you can access more than one agency.

## Forgotten username or password

Contact your **agency administrator**. They can reset you to a temporary password. Then follow [First-time login](#first-time-login) to set a permanent password.

Thin Line support does not reset passwords for end users when your agency owns local administration — see [Support](../support/README.md).

## Log out

1. Click your **avatar** in the upper-right header.
2. Choose **Log Out** / **Sign Out** (use the label shown in your build).
3. On shared computers, **close the browser window** after sign-out.
