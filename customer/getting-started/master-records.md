# Master records

Using the right-side master record rail for shared people, places, and property.

## What master records are

Master records are shared indexes used across Thin Line modules:

| Master type | Typical use |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Persons** | People linked to incidents, citations, court cases, bookings, and more |
| **Organizations** | Businesses and other organizations |
| **Vehicles** | Vehicles referenced on records |
| **Property** | Property items (distinct from Evidence room inventory workflows) |
| **Locations** | Addresses / places used on calls and records |

They appear as icons on the **far-right rail** in the RMS shell. The rail is usually hidden in live CAD or Jail Command Center specialty modes — switch to **RMS** if you need master search while those boards are open.

## Search or add

1. Click a master type (for example **Persons**).
2. A **slide-out** opens on the right.
3. Enter search terms. Results update as you provide enough information.
4. Open a result to view or edit, or choose **Add** when no match exists and your role allows create.

Keep person and vehicle masters clean: search before adding so the agency does not create duplicates.

## Feedback

The lower part of the right rail includes **Feedback**. Use it to send a note to Thin Line about the product. For urgent outages or access problems, use your agency’s support process as well — see [Support](../support/README.md).
