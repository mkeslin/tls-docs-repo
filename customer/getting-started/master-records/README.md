# Master records

Shared **Persons**, **Organizations**, **Vehicles**, **Property**, and **Locations** used across Thin Line modules.

## What master records are

Master records are the agency’s shared indexes. Modules (incidents, citations, warrants, jail bookings, and more) **link** to masters instead of inventing a disconnected copy of every person or plate each time.

| Master type | Typical use |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Persons** | People on incidents, citations, court cases, bookings, warrants |
| **Organizations** | Businesses and other organizations |
| **Vehicles** | Vehicles on stops, incidents, and related records |
| **Property** | Property items (distinct from day-to-day Evidence room custody — see [Evidence](../../rms/evidence/README.md)) |
| **Locations** | Addresses and places on calls and reports |

## Where to find them

Icons on the **far-right rail** in the RMS shell open a slide-out for that master type. The rail is usually hidden in live CAD or Jail Command Center modes — switch to **RMS** if you need master search from those boards.

The lower right rail also includes **Feedback** (product comments) — see [Support — Feedback](../../support/feedback.md).
