# Persons

![Masters — open Persons from the right rail](images/getting-started-modules-rail.png)

The shared **Persons** master for people across Thin Line.

## Open and edit

1. Right rail → **Persons**.
2. Search or add (see [Search and add](search-and-add.md)).
3. Open the person card. Common tabs:

| Tab | Contents |
|-----|----------|
| **General** | Name, DOB/DOD, identifiers (SSN, DL, FBI #, SID, agency IDs), demographics, aliases, phones, emails, unknown-person flags |
| **Records** | Module records linked to this person (incidents, citations, and more) |
| **Associations** | Linked locations, vehicles, property, organizations, other persons |
| **SMTs** | Scars, marks, tattoos, and similar descriptors |
| **Notes** | Notes on the master |
| **Attachments** | Files and avatar photo |
| **History** | Audit-style changes (when you can access RMS audit) |

## Attachments and avatar

There is no separate “Photos” product name — use **Attachments**. The first or designated attachment can serve as the person’s **avatar** in the UI.

## Tags and cues

Person cards may show tags (for example an active **warrant** cue) via master tagging. Treat tags as alerts to investigate — open [Warrants](../../rms/warrants/README.md) or Records for detail.

## Tips

- Prefer one master person per real person; merge duplicates instead of maintaining two files — see [Duplicates and merge](duplicates-and-merge.md).
- Update identifiers (DL, DOB) carefully; modules store snapshots that may not auto-refresh — see [Linking and snapshots](linking-and-snapshots.md).
- Unknown / provisional persons should be upgraded to a full master when identity is confirmed.
