# Application shell

How the Thin Line window is laid out, and what **RMS**, **CAD**, and **JAIL** mean in the header.

## The five zones

In the standard (classic) layout you will see these areas:

| Zone | Where | What it is for |
|------|-------|----------------|
| **Top header bar** | Across the top | Product mode, agency, Offense Search, Help, Admin, user menu |
| **Left module rail** | Far left icons | Switch between modules (Incidents, Citations, Court, and so on) |
| **Module menu** | Wider panel next to the rail | Search, Add, Reports, and other actions for the active module |
| **Main content** | Center | The page you are working on (search grids, record details, boards) |
| **Master records rail** | Far right | Quick search/add for Persons, Organizations, Vehicles, Property, Locations |

Security or system banners may appear above the main content when your agency or Thin Line posts them.

CAD and the Jail Command Center use specialty shells: the left RMS module rail and right master rail may be hidden or reduced while you work in those modes. Switch back to **RMS** in the header to return to the full records shell.

## Product modes (header toggle)

The header can show **RMS**, **CAD**, and **JAIL** (only modes your permissions allow):

| Mode | Plain-language purpose |
|------|------------------------|
| **RMS** | Records management — modules, dashboard, admin, help, analytics |
| **CAD** | Live computer-aided dispatch console |
| **JAIL** | Jail Command Center and jail booking / observation workflows |

These are **product modes**, not the same thing as a single left-rail module. For example, Court Violations is an RMS module; Jail Intake is available from RMS navigation and also from JAIL mode.

## Main content habits

- Most pages show a **page title** at the top of the content area (not a full breadcrumb trail on desktop).
- Record screens often use **tabs** (General, Status, History, and so on) inside the page — those tabs are local to that record, not global navigation.
- There is **no single global search** for the whole product. Use the active module’s **Search**, **Offense Search** in the header, or a **master record** search on the right.

## Collapse the left nav

Use the **hamburger** control at the left of the header to collapse or expand the left module rail when you need more room for the page.
