# Analytics

![Analytics reports](images/analytics-reports.png)

Customer guides for **Analytics** — charts and analytics reports across CAD, RMS, code enforcement, and court (when licensed and permitted).

## What Analytics is

Analytics is a **read-only insight** area. It does not replace entering incidents, citations, or court cases. Use it for volume trends, activity pictures, and scheduled analytics reports your claims allow.

## Who this guide is for

- Supervisors and command staff reviewing activity
- Analysts / records staff who run analytics reports
- Court administrators reviewing court analytics (when enabled)
