# Analytics reports

![Analytics reports](images/analytics-reports.png)

1. Open **Analytics** → **Reports** (shown when you have incident access in the current product wiring).
2. Choose the analytics report and parameters.
3. Run the report and retrieve output from the report flow your agency uses (including **Report History** on the Dashboard when applicable).

For statutory / transmission-style exports (IBRS, racial profiling, DPS conviction, OCA, state quarterly), use [Import/Export](../import-export/README.md) or Court reports as documented there — not Analytics Charts alone.
