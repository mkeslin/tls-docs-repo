# Opening Analytics

1. Open **Analytics** from the left module rail (requires analytics access).
2. Choose a destination from the Analytics menu.
3. Set date ranges and filters on the chart or report screen.
4. Export or print only if your screen offers it and your agency policy allows.

Menu items appear only when related modules/claims are enabled (for example, Call Analytics needs CAD access).
