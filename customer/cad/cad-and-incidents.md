# CAD and incidents

How live calls relate to RMS incident reports.

## Typical handoff

1. Work the call in **CAD** until enough facts exist for a report (or your policy requires one).
2. Use the product action your build provides to create or open a related **Incident** (wording varies by version).
3. Complete the incident in RMS — narratives, offenses, involved persons — per [Incidents](../rms/incidents/README.md).
4. Keep the call and incident identifiers aligned so records staff can find both later in [CAD Records](records/README.md) and Incident Search.

## Tips

- Not every call needs an incident — follow agency reportable-event rules.
- Master persons / locations should be linked consistently on both the call and the incident.
