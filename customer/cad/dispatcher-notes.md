# Dispatcher notes

Agency-wide notes on the live CAD console — separate from notes on a single call sheet.

## Open Dispatcher Notes

1. Open [live CAD](live-cad-overview.md).
2. Open the floating **Dispatcher Notes** control (bottom sheet / FAB style control).
3. Read recent notes; toggle **View Unit Notes** when you need unit-oriented note visibility.

Requires full CAD modify to add notes.

## Add a dispatcher note

1. Open Dispatcher Notes.
2. Enter text in **Add dispatcher note...**.
3. Save / add per the control on the sheet.
4. Confirm other consoles can see the note as your agency’s sync allows.

## Dispatcher notes vs call notes

| Kind | Where | Use for |
|------|-------|---------|
| **Call note** | Call sheet → [Call sheet activity](call-sheet-activity.md) | Facts about **this** call |
| **Dispatcher note** | Floating Dispatcher Notes | Board-wide messages (BOLOs, channel info, shift notices) |

## Print / history

Printing dispatcher notes typically routes to historical **Dispatcher Note Search** in [CAD Records](records/dispatcher-notes.md).

## Tips

- Keep BOLO / safety notes factual and time-stamped in the text when policy requires it.
- Do not put evidence-chain narrative only in dispatcher notes — put case facts on the call and the incident.
