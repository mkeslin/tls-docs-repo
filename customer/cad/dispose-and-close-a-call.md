# Dispose and close a call

Finish the call when units are clear and a disposition is set.

## Clear every unit first

1. Open the call (card or call sheet).
2. **Clear** each assigned unit ([Assign and clear units](assign-and-clear-units.md)).
3. Watch the header / unit strip until **no active units** remain.

Disposition typically appears only after all units are cleared.

## Set Disposition

1. On the call sheet header, choose **Disposition** (agency code list).
2. Select the disposition that matches your agency’s outcome codes.
3. Save (selection usually saves on change).
4. The call is treated as **closed** and moves toward the **Closed Calls** list (recent window).

Disposition codes are agency-configured — train the codes your tenant uses, not a universal list.

## What “close” does *not* mean

| Action | Result |
|--------|--------|
| **Close Call Sheet** / X on the right panel | Hides the sheet; call can still be open |
| **Clear** one unit | That unit leaves; call may still be open |
| **Disposition** after all units cleared | Call is disposed / closed |

## After the call is closed

- Recent closed calls: expand **Closed Calls** on the Calls panel (~24 hours).
- Older research: [CAD Records — Call sheets](records/call-sheets.md).
- If a report is required and not yet created: [Related incidents and citations](related-incidents-and-citations.md).

## Tips

- Do not dispose while units are still showing active on the call.
- If Disposition never appears, confirm every unit is Cleared and you have modify rights.
- Reopening or correcting closed-call times/notes may offer More Options — use only per supervisor policy.
