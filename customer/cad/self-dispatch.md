# Self-dispatch

Officer-initiated CAD from the Dashboard when your agency enables mobile / self-dispatch.

## Open Self-Dispatch

1. Sign in with an account that can access CAD self-dispatch.
2. Open [Dashboard](../getting-started/dashboard.md) → **Self-Dispatch** (menu item and/or home tab).
3. Confirm you are assigned to a **CAD unit**. If you see that you are not assigned to a unit, stop and ask your administrator to link your user/officer to a unit.

Self-dispatch is **not** the three-panel full console. For the dispatcher board, use header **CAD** ([Live CAD overview](live-cad-overview.md)).

## What you can do

| Action | Notes |
|--------|-------|
| See your unit and open calls | Focused on your assignment |
| **Add Call** | When you are Available, not already on a call, and have modify rights |
| **Assign** yourself | Assigns **your** unit (must be Available) |
| **En Route** / **Arrived** / **Clear From Call** | For your unit on the call (when those controls are enabled) |
| Unit notes | Notes panel for your unit context |

You do **not** drag-assign other units or run agency-wide [Dispatcher notes](dispatcher-notes.md) the way a full console dispatcher does.

## Typical officer flow

1. Set or confirm unit status **Available** ([Unit status](unit-status.md) concepts apply).
2. **Add Call** or select an open call you should take.
3. **Assign** your unit to the call.
4. Update **En Route** → **Arrived** as you respond (if shown).
5. Add people / notes as your build allows on the self-dispatch UI.
6. **Clear From Call** when complete.
7. Create or open an incident when required — often from the full call sheet / related records path, or via records after the fact ([Related incidents and citations](related-incidents-and-citations.md)).

## When to use the full console instead

- You are the dispatcher for the agency
- You need to assign multiple units or clear another unit
- You need Disposition on a multi-unit call you are coordinating

## Tips

- Agency policy may still require calling into dispatch — self-dispatch is a product capability, not a policy override.
- If Self-Dispatch is missing from the Dashboard, you lack claims or `cadMobile` / self-dispatch is not enabled.
- Historical research: [CAD Records](records/README.md).
