# Self-dispatch

Officer-initiated CAD work when your agency enables self-dispatch (often from the **Dashboard**).

## Typical steps

1. Sign in with an account that can self-dispatch.
2. From **Dashboard** (or the self-dispatch entry your agency configured), start a self-dispatch / call action.
3. Enter location and call type.
4. Confirm you are assigned as the unit on the call.
5. Update status as you arrive and clear.
6. Create or link an [Incident](../rms/incidents/README.md) when a report is required — see [CAD and incidents](cad-and-incidents.md).

## Tips

- Self-dispatch is not a substitute for calling into dispatch when your agency policy requires a dispatcher.
- If the self-dispatch control is missing, you may lack claims or the agency may not enable that path.
