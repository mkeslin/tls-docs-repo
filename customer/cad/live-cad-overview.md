# Live CAD overview

Using the real-time CAD console.

## Open live CAD

1. Confirm you have CAD full access and CAD is enabled for your agency.
2. In the header, switch product mode to **CAD** (or open the CAD destination your build provides).
3. The console shows **units** and **calls** side by side (layout may vary slightly by theme).

## Typical dispatcher flow

1. Create or select a **call for service**.
2. Enter location, call type, and priority fields as shown.
3. Assign or update **units** on the call.
4. Maintain notes and people/vehicles on the call sheet as information arrives.
5. Update unit status as officers respond, arrive, and clear.
6. Close or dispose the call per your agency codes when complete.

## Tips

- Always confirm the **agency** in the header before creating calls.
- Live CAD is operational — use [CAD Records](records/README.md) for historical research so you do not disrupt the console.
- If you only need history, stay in RMS and open **CAD Records**.
