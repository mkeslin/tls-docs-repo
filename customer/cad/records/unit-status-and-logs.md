# Unit status and logs

## Unit Status & Duty Logs

1. Open **CAD Records** → **Unit Status & Duty Logs**.
2. Search by unit, officer, or date range.
3. Review status / duty history returned by the search.

## Unit Logs

1. Open **CAD Records** → **Unit Logs**.
2. Search the period and unit of interest.
3. Open log rows for detail as needed.

## Tips

- Pair unit history with [Call sheets](call-sheets.md) when reconstructing a shift or incident.
