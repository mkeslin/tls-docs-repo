# Warrants workshop

**Audience:** Records / warrants officers, patrol who record service, and court staff who coordinate FTA/CPF  
**Goal:** Search and maintain a warrant, record a service attempt, and understand court-owned FTA/CPF vs local add  
**Typical length:** ~2 hours  
**Environment:** Training tenant with sample local and (if possible) court-owned warrants  

## Prerequisites

- [New user orientation](new-user-orientation.md) completed (or equivalent)
- Warrants (WAR) access; Court access if practicing Issue FTA/CPF

## Agenda

| Block | Minutes | Guide | Exercise |
|-------|--------:|-------|----------|
| Overview | 10 | [Warrants overview](../rms/warrants/README.md) | Local vs COURT OWNED |
| Search | 15 | [Search](../rms/warrants/search.md) | Find active warrant; note court-generated icon if present |
| Add (local) | 20 | [Add](../rms/warrants/add.md) · [General](../rms/warrants/general.md) · [Dates](../rms/warrants/dates.md) | Create local warrant; tag person |
| Offenses | 10 | [Offenses](../rms/warrants/offenses.md) | Add a charge line |
| Service | 25 | [Service attempts](../rms/warrants/service-attempts.md) | Add attempt; discuss COMPLETED / attestation |
| Court FTA/CPF | 25 | [Court-owned FTA and CPF](../rms/warrants/court-owned-fta-cpf.md) · [Court FTA](../court/fta-warrants-bonds.md) | Demo issue/recall **or** walk sample court-owned warrant |
| Print / history | 10 | [Print, attachments, history](../rms/warrants/print-attachments-history.md) | Print or open History |
| Wrap | 5 | [Support](../support/README.md) | Questions |

## Exit checklist

- [ ] Search and open a warrant; identify COURT OWNED when present
- [ ] Add or edit a local warrant with master person + offense
- [ ] Record a service attempt
- [ ] Explain who issues/recalls FTA/CPF vs who records service
- [ ] Know where Warrants docs live for self-serve
