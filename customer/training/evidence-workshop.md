# Evidence workshop

**Audience:** Property room / evidence staff and officers who book evidence from incidents  
**Goal:** Take property into custody from an incident, record IN/OUT/TR history, and use Search / Inventory / Audit  
**Typical length:** ~2 hours  
**Environment:** Training tenant with a sample incident  

## Prerequisites

- [New user orientation](new-user-orientation.md) completed (or equivalent)
- Incidents + Evidence access; modify evidence for hands-on intake
- Ideally [Incidents workshop](incidents-workshop.md) first

## Agenda

| Block | Minutes | Guide | Exercise |
|-------|--------:|-------|----------|
| Overview | 10 | [Evidence overview](../rms/evidence/README.md) | Module vs incident Property/Evidence |
| From incident | 20 | [From incident property](../rms/evidence/from-incident-property.md) · [Wizard](../rms/evidence/add-evidence-wizard.md) | Add property; take into custody as evidence |
| Chain of custody | 25 | [Chain of custody](../rms/evidence/chain-of-custody.md) | Add TR; discuss OUT |
| Search & inventory | 20 | [Search](../rms/evidence/search.md) · [Inventory](../rms/evidence/inventory.md) | Find by evidence #; open Inventory |
| Audit & print | 20 | [Inventory audit](../rms/evidence/inventory-audit.md) · [Print](../rms/evidence/print-and-labels.md) | Run sample audit; print label or CoC |
| Masters & permissions | 15 | [Master property](../rms/evidence/master-property-and-evidence.md) · [Permissions](../rms/evidence/permissions.md) | View from master; who can edit history |
| Wrap | 5 | [Support](../support/README.md) | Questions |

## Exit checklist

- [ ] Create or promote evidence from an incident
- [ ] Add at least one custody history event
- [ ] Find the item in Search and Inventory
- [ ] Explain OUT vs “disposition” language in the product
- [ ] Know where Evidence docs live for self-serve
