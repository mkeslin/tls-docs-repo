# Role plan: Jail intake

**Audience:** Booking / intake officers at a jail facility  
**Typical length:** Orientation + Jail workshop Session A (or combined half day)

## Goals

- Create bookings and complete required intake steps
- Accept into Custody at a facility agency
- Know Delete booking vs Release
- Hand off cleanly to the floor board

## Path

| Order | Curriculum / guide | Length |
|------:|--------------------|--------|
| 1 | [Shared foundation](README.md#shared-foundation-all-roles) | ~half day |
| 2 | [Jail](../../jail/README.md) overview + [Getting around](../../jail/getting-around.md) | 30 min |
| 3 | [Jail operations workshop](../jail-operations-workshop.md) — **Session A — Intake** (or combined) | 2–4 h |

## Claims to verify

- Jail Intake Access + Modify
- Facility (jail) agency in header for Accept
- Master person search/add

## Exit checklist

- [ ] Create Booking wizard through **Create Booking**
- [ ] Mark required steps complete; defer one conditional with reason
- [ ] Accept into Custody; find episode on Command Center
- [ ] Delete only a pre-accept draft in training (policy permitting)
