# Role plan: Court clerk / cashier

**Audience:** Court clerks, cashiers, and court admin who process cases and take payments  
**Typical length:** Orientation + half day Court (+ finance workshop if they also close deposits)

## Goals

- Navigate Court search, queues, and case lifecycle
- Apply and accept payments correctly
- Handle pleas/judgment/calendar tasks per local assignment
- Know when to hand off to finance or collections

## Path

| Order | Curriculum / guide | Length |
|------:|--------------------|--------|
| 1 | [Shared foundation](README.md#shared-foundation-all-roles) | ~half day |
| 2 | Journey: [Court payment to accounting](../../getting-started/journeys/court-payment-to-accounting.md) — cashier portion | 30 min |
| 3 | [Court clerk workshop](../court-clerk-workshop.md) | ~half day |
| 4 | [Court finance workshop](../court-finance-workshop.md) — only if they post deposits / remittances | optional |

## Claims to verify

- Court Violations access/modify
- Payment apply/accept paths used by your queues
- Work queue access

## Exit checklist

- [ ] Open a case from search and from a work queue
- [ ] Apply and accept a training payment; explain pending vs accepted
- [ ] Complete one non-payment clerk task (plea, setting, or queue item per agency)
- [ ] Know not to double-post collections remittances in Court
