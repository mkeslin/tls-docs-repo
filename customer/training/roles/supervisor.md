# Role plan: Supervisor

**Audience:** Sergeants, records supervisors, court supervisors, jail supervisors who approve work and coach staff  
**Typical length:** Foundation + their product workshops + coaching on queues/approvals

## Goals

- Understand the staff role plans they supervise
- Perform approvals / QA in their product
- Use Dashboard Tasks and Report History
- Escalate correctly (agency admin vs Thin Line)

## Path

| Order | Curriculum / guide | Length |
|------:|--------------------|--------|
| 1 | [Shared foundation](README.md#shared-foundation-all-roles) | ~half day |
| 2 | Staff role plan(s) they supervise — sit in or review | varies |
| 3 | Product workshop for their area (Incidents / Court / Jail / CAD / Evidence) | varies |
| 4 | [Dashboard](../../getting-started/dashboard.md) Tasks + Report History | 30 min |
| 5 | [FAQ](../../support/faq.md) · [Troubleshooting](../../support/troubleshooting.md) | 30 min |

## Exit checklist

- [ ] Approve or return a training record in their product
- [ ] Find work on Dashboard Tasks
- [ ] Name the role plan for each direct report type
- [ ] Know when to use [Support](../../support/README.md)
