# Training by role

Recommended learning paths by **job role**. Each plan lists required workshops and guides in order — use them as the teaching script; do not invent product steps that are not documented.

## How to use

1. Pick the role closest to the trainee’s job (combine plans if someone wears two hats).
2. Complete **shared foundation** once per person.
3. Deliver role workshops in a training tenant.
4. Sign off against the role exit checklist before go-live.

Agency administrators assign [claims](../../admin/users-roles-claims.md) to match the role — training without permissions wastes time.
