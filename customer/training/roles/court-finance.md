# Role plan: Court finance / collections

**Audience:** Staff who post deposit/revenue batches, reconcile card payouts, and/or run collections remittance  
**Typical length:** Court clerk basics (if new) + [Court finance workshop](../court-finance-workshop.md)

## Goals

- Trace Apply/Accept → Create & Post Batches
- Reconcile online payouts cautiously (VerifyOnly)
- Refer/recall and remit collections without double-posting
- Run assigned Import/Export court packs

## Path

| Order | Curriculum / guide | Length |
|------:|--------------------|--------|
| 1 | [Shared foundation](README.md#shared-foundation-all-roles) | ~half day |
| 2 | [Court — Payments](../../court/payments.md) (read + demo) | 45 min |
| 3 | Journey: [Court payment to accounting](../../getting-started/journeys/court-payment-to-accounting.md) | 30 min |
| 4 | [Court finance workshop](../court-finance-workshop.md) | ~half day |
| 5 | [Import/Export](../../import-export/README.md) packs they own | 1 h |

## Claims to verify

- Accounting access (court agency)
- Collections Access and Modify as needed
- Court report / Import-Export claims for DPS/OCA/SQ

## Exit checklist

- [ ] Create & Post a deposit batch (or explain empty pending)
- [ ] Describe revenue allocation purpose
- [ ] Enter or preview a collections remittance CSV
- [ ] Name tools that are Support/finance-lead only (Cleanup, VerifyAndHeal)
