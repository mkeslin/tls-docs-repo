# Court finance workshop

**Audience:** Court cashiers, court finance, collections clerks  
**Goal:** Apply/accept payments, post deposit and revenue batches, handle a collections remittance, and run one statutory export  
**Typical length:** ~half day  
**Environment:** Training tenant (court agency) with sample payable cases

## Prerequisites

- [New user orientation](new-user-orientation.md)
- [Court clerk workshop](court-clerk-workshop.md) recommended first
- [Journey: Court payment to accounting](../getting-started/journeys/court-payment-to-accounting.md)
- Accounts with Court + Accounting access; Collections modify if training remittance

## Agenda

| Block | Minutes | Guide | Exercise |
|-------|--------:|-------|----------|
| Court pay | 40 | [Payments](../court/payments.md) · [From Court payments](../accounting/from-court-payments.md) | Apply + accept on a sample case; print final receipt |
| Deposit | 35 | [Deposit batches](../accounting/deposit-batches.md) | Create & Post Batches; find the batch |
| Revenue | 20 | [Revenue allocation](../accounting/revenue-allocation.md) | Create & Post allocation (if pending exists) |
| Online / reconcile | 25 | [Online payments](../accounting/online-payments-and-payouts.md) · [Reconciliation](../accounting/reconciliation-and-disputes.md) | Open ledger + discuss VerifyOnly vs heal |
| Collections | 40 | [Refer and recall](../collections/refer-and-recall.md) · [Payment entry](../collections/payment-entry.md) · [Disbursements](../collections/disbursements.md) | Refer (if eligible) or walk portfolio; preview CSV columns; open disbursement report |
| Import/Export | 30 | [IBRS](../import-export/ibrs.md) or [DPS](../import-export/dps-conviction-report.md) · [Checklist](../import-export/data-quality-checklist.md) | Create a **training** transmission; do not submit externally |
| Wrap | 10 | — | Failure points from the journey |

## Exit checklist

- [ ] Apply and accept a payment; explain pending vs accepted
- [ ] Create & Post a deposit batch (or explain empty pending)
- [ ] Name when revenue allocation is used
- [ ] Describe collections refer vs remittance vs disbursement (no double-post)
- [ ] Know where Import/Export lives and which pack their job owns
- [ ] Know VerifyAndHeal and Payment Cleanup are not casual tools
