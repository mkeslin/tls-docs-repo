# Jail operations workshop

**Audience:** Booking / intake staff and Command Center (floor) staff — can split into two shorter sessions  
**Goal:** Complete a booking through Accept Into Custody; house, check, and release/transfer on the board  
**Typical length:** ~3–4 hours (half day), or 2 × ~2 hours (Intake then Command Center)  
**Environment:** Training facility with jail locations configured  

## Prerequisites

- [New user orientation](new-user-orientation.md) completed (or equivalent)
- Jail Intake and/or Jail Command Center access
- Training locations and schedules configured

## Agenda — combined half day

| Block | Minutes | Guide | Exercise |
|-------|--------:|-------|----------|
| Jail overview | 15 | [Jail overview](../jail/README.md) | Intake vs Command Center |
| Getting around | 15 | [Getting around](../jail/getting-around.md) | Open board + Booking Search |
| Start booking | 20 | [Start a booking](../jail/start-a-booking.md) | Add Booking from JCC |
| Intake steps | 45 | [Complete intake steps](../jail/intake-steps.md) | Complete required steps; defer one conditional with reason |
| Accept | 20 | [Accept Into Custody](../jail/accept-into-custody.md) | Accept; find episode on board |
| Command Center | 25 | [Command Center basics](../jail/command-center.md) | Roster vs Layout; filters |
| Housing | 25 | [Housing and moves](../jail/housing-and-moves.md) | Move to a cell / location |
| Checks & meds | 25 | [Cell checks](../jail/observation-and-cell-checks.md) · [Flags & medication](../jail/flags-alerts-medication.md) | Walk Pass Runner or work queue task |
| Release or transfer | 25 | [Release](../jail/release.md) · [Offsite transfer](../jail/offsite-transfer.md) | Complete one path in training |
| Reports & wrap | 15 | [Reports](../jail/reports.md) · [Support](../support/README.md) | Print roster or booking PDF |

## Split option

| Session | Focus | Guides |
|---------|-------|--------|
| **A — Intake** | Booking through Accept | Start booking → Accept Into Custody |
| **B — Floor** | Board, moves, checks, release | Command Center → Release / transfer |

## Exit checklist

- [ ] Create a booking and reach Ready for acceptance
- [ ] Accept Into Custody and locate the episode
- [ ] Complete one housing move
- [ ] Know where Jail topic docs live for self-serve later
