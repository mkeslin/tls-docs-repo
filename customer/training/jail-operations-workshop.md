# Jail operations workshop

**Audience:** Booking / intake and Command Center (floor) staff — split by [role plans](roles/jail-intake.md) / [jail floor](roles/jail-floor.md)  
**Goal:** Complete a booking through Accept into Custody; house, check, med-log, and release/transfer on the board  
**Typical length:** ~3–4 hours combined, or 2 × ~2 hours (Intake then Floor)  
**Environment:** Training **jail facility** agency with locations configured ([Manage locations](../jail/manage-locations.md))

## Prerequisites

- [New user orientation](new-user-orientation.md)
- Jail Intake and/or Observation claims matching the session
- Header agency = jail facility (required for Accept)

## Agenda — combined half day

| Block | Minutes | Guide | Exercise |
|-------|--------:|-------|----------|
| Overview | 10 | [Jail](../jail/README.md) | Intake vs **JAIL** Command Center |
| Getting around | 15 | [Getting around](../jail/getting-around.md) | Open board + Booking Search |
| Start booking | 20 | [Start a booking](../jail/start-a-booking.md) | Wizard through **Create Booking** |
| Intake steps | 40 | [Complete intake steps](../jail/intake-steps.md) | Mark Complete; defer one conditional |
| Accept | 20 | [Accept into Custody](../jail/accept-into-custody.md) | Accept; find episode |
| Command Center | 20 | [Command Center basics](../jail/command-center.md) | Roster vs Layout; heartbeat filters |
| Housing | 20 | [Housing and moves](../jail/housing-and-moves.md) | **Move** to a cell |
| Passes | 25 | [Cell checks](../jail/observation-and-cell-checks.md) | **Start Pass** → **Complete Pass** |
| Flags & meds | 20 | [Flags & medication](../jail/flags-alerts-medication.md) | Log administration or set Suicide Watch (policy) |
| Release / transfer | 25 | [Release](../jail/release.md) · [Offsite transfer](../jail/offsite-transfer.md) | One path in training |
| Reports & wrap | 10 | [Reports](../jail/reports.md) | Roster or booking PDF |

## Split option

| Session | Focus | Role plan |
|---------|-------|-----------|
| **A — Intake** | Booking through Accept | [Jail intake](roles/jail-intake.md) |
| **B — Floor** | Board, moves, passes, release | [Jail floor](roles/jail-floor.md) |

## Exit checklist

- [ ] Create a booking and reach Ready for Acceptance
- [ ] Accept into Custody and locate the episode
- [ ] Complete one housing move
- [ ] Start/complete a pass **or** log a medication (per role)
- [ ] Know [Jail](../jail/README.md) docs and [role plans](roles/README.md) for self-serve
