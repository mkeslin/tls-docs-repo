# Revenue allocation

Distribute court trust liability into revenue using fee allocation percentages.

## What revenue allocation does

A **Revenue Allocation Batch** groups transaction sets that still hold court trust credits and, when posted, debits court trust liability and credits revenue accounts per your fee allocation setup.

Same posting pattern as deposits: **one batch per agency per date** is the normal expectation.

## Create and post

1. Open **Accounting** → **Revenue Allocation Batch**.
2. Choose **Create & Post Batches**.
3. Review and select pending sets.
4. Confirm **Create & Post Batches**.

Empty pending means there is nothing waiting to allocate — confirm deposits and fee setup first.

## Search and void

Search by status and dates as on the deposit screen. **Void Batch** (with **Void Reason**) reverses when your process requires it.

## Tips

- Fee allocation % lives on fee/account setup — see [Accounts, fees, and plans](accounts-fees-and-plans.md).
- Run revenue allocation on the cadence your city auditor expects (often same day as or next day after deposit).
