# Dashboard and batches

## Dashboard

1. Open **Accounting** from the left rail (court agency + accounting access).
2. Choose **Dashboard**.
3. Use the dashboard tiles / summaries to see work that needs attention (deposits, exceptions — as configured).

## Deposit Batch

1. Open **Deposit Batch**.
2. Search existing batches or create a new deposit batch for the drawer / day you are closing.
3. Include accepted payments per your agency process.
4. Complete and post the batch actions shown on the screen.

## Revenue Allocation Batch

1. Open **Revenue Allocation Batch**.
2. Search or create a batch that allocates deposited revenue to accounts / funds.
3. Review lines for correctness before finalizing.

## Tips

- Only **accepted** court payments belong in deposit close-out — see [Court — Payments](../court/payments.md).
- Keep batch dates aligned with physical bank deposits.
