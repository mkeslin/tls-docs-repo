# Ledgers and reconciliation

## Payment Ledger

1. Open **Payment Ledger**.
2. Search payments by date, method, case, or other filters shown.
3. Open a row when you need line-level detail.

## Payout Batch

1. Open **Payout Batch**.
2. Search or create payout batches for card / electronic settlement to the agency.
3. Complete batch review before treating funds as settled.

## Payment Reconciliation / Payout Reconciliation

1. Open **Payment Reconciliation** or **Payout Reconciliation**.
2. Search the period you are balancing.
3. Resolve exceptions using the tools on the screen; escalate unresolved processor mismatches to Thin Line Support with dates and batch ids.

## Charge Disputes

1. Open **Charge Disputes**.
2. Search Stripe (or processor) charge disputes affecting court payments.
3. Follow your agency finance process; coordinate with Support when the dispute tools need configuration help.

## Tips

- Reconcile on a fixed cadence (daily / weekly) so exceptions stay small.
- Do not “fix” ledger history by re-entering court payments — correct at the source case when possible.
