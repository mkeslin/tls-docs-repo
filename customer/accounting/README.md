# Accounting

Customer guides for the **Accounting (ACC)** module — court back-office deposits, ledgers, payouts, reconciliations, bonds, fees, and payment plans.

## What Accounting is

**Accounting** is the finance workspace for **court agencies**. Front-desk cashiers usually **apply and accept payments** on court cases ([Court — Payments](../court/payments.md)); Accounting is where deposits, revenue allocation, ledgers, payouts, and reconciliations are managed.

The Accounting rail appears when you have accounting access **and** you are in a court agency context.

## Who this guide is for

- Court clerks / finance staff who close out payments
- Staff who reconcile card payouts and disputes
- Administrators who maintain fee schedules and accounts
