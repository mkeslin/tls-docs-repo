## Working-session objectives

This meeting is meant to help Thin Line and your agency:

- Understand **current workflows** as they actually run  
- Identify what should be **preserved**  
- Identify what should be **improved**  
- Validate **product fit** for how you work  
- Identify **migration**, **hardware**, and **integration** needs at a discovery level  
- Agree on **clear next steps** and owners  

We will not try to finish every detail today. Firm migration scope, pricing, and some technical answers come later when the right people and materials are ready.
