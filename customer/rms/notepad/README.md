# Notepad

![Notepad search](images/rms-notepad-search.png)

Customer guides for the **Notepad (PAD)** module — field contacts / notepad records and **Lost & Found** search.

## What Notepad is

A **notepad** (field contact) captures a contact or observation that is not a full incident report: people, organizations, vehicles, property, notes, and attachments. Agencies use it for intelligence-style contacts, follow-ups, and similar short documentation.

| Use Notepad when… | Prefer another module when… |
|-------------------|-----------------------------|
| Documenting a field contact or observation | You need offenses, arrests, or approval workflow → **Incidents** |
| Linking people / vehicles / property lightly | You need a scheduled property check → **Close Patrol** |
| Searching lost & found items tied to notepads | You need evidence custody → **Evidence** |

Nav abbreviation is **PAD** (module key may show as **FCS**).

## Who this guide is for

- Officers creating field contacts
- Records / detectives searching notepads and lost & found
- Supervisors reviewing attachments and history
