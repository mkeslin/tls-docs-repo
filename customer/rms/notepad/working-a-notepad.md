# Working a notepad

## Tabs

| Tab | Purpose |
|-----|---------|
| **General** | When/where, type/status, narrative summary fields as shown |
| **Persons** | Involved people (master links) |
| **Organizations** | Businesses / groups |
| **Property** | Property items on the contact |
| **Vehicles** | Vehicles on the contact |
| **Attachments** | Photos and files |
| **History** | Audit history (when permitted) |

## Typical workflow

1. Open the notepad.
2. Complete **General** (status and follow-up habits your agency uses).
3. Add **Persons**, **Vehicles**, and **Property** as needed — prefer existing masters.
4. Attach photos if they support the contact.
5. Save; return later from Search when follow-up is due.

## Tips

- Escalate to an [Incident](../incidents/README.md) when the contact becomes a reportable offense or arrest.
- Keep statuses consistent so supervisors can find open follow-ups.
