# Dates

Important dates and court-related timing on the warrant **Dates** tab.

## Typical date fields

| Field | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Entered** | When the warrant was entered |
| **Issued** | When the warrant was issued (with Issued By when shown) |
| **Recalled** | When recalled (with Recalled By when shown) |
| **Expired** | Expiration when used |
| **Court date** | Related court appearance / show-cause style date when populated |

Exact labels follow your agency configuration. Court-driven FTA/CPF actions often set or update these dates when the warrant is issued or recalled from Court.

## Tips

- Keep issued / recalled dates aligned with paper and court orders.
- Expired active warrants should be reviewed under your agency’s purge / recall policy — do not leave stale **Active** statuses.
- Court date on the warrant complements — it does not replace — the court violation calendar.
