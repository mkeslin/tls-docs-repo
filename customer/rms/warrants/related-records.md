# Related records

How warrants connect to incidents, jail, and master persons.

## Module record groups

From warrant detail, use **associated records / record groups** (same pattern as other RMS modules) to link related **incidents**, **citations**, **notepads**, and similar records when your agency associates them. There is no dedicated “incident tab” on the warrant — associations are the linker.

## Master person

The warrant **General** tab tags a **master person**. Keep that link accurate so:

- Search by name works
- Jail intake can reference warrant identity
- Court-owned warrants stay tied to the correct defendant

See [Master records](../../getting-started/master-records.md).

## Jail intake

Jail booking / custody steps may capture warrant number, issuing agency, confirmation, and extradition-style fields. That is **Jail Intake** data entry — it does not replace creating or serving the warrant in WAR. See [Jail](../../jail/README.md).

## Court

Court violations remain the source of truth for FTA/CPF **issue / recall / execute**. Cross-link: [Court-owned FTA and CPF](court-owned-fta-cpf.md).

## Citations

There is no everyday “citation → warrant” button in current CIT UI. Warrants from charging usually go through court processes or local WAR add — not a citation conversion wizard.
