# Search warrants

Find and open warrant records from the Warrants module.

## Open Search

1. In RMS, open **Warrants** from the left module rail.
2. Choose **Search** (default landing for the module).

## Common filters

| Filter | Use |
|--------|-----|
| **Warrant number** | Exact or quick-jump style lookup |
| **Name** | Subject on the warrant |
| **Workflow** | Draft / other workflow buckets when used |
| **Warrant type** | Agency type codes (including court-created AR / CPF styles) |
| **Warrant status** | Active, cleared, recalled, and related codes |
| **Jurisdiction** | Issuing / jurisdiction codes |
| **Offense** | Charge on the warrant |
| **Record date** | Date range |

Open a row to warrant **detail**. Results may show an indicator when the warrant was **generated from a court violation**.

## Tips

- Confirm the correct **agency** in the header — multi-agency users often miss court-owned vs LE agency context.
- Use status **Active** (or your agency’s equivalent) for serviceable warrants.
- Court-owned warrants may appear for your PD even when the owning agency is the court — see [Court-owned FTA and CPF](court-owned-fta-cpf.md).
