# Print, attachments, and history

Reports, files, and audit trail on a warrant.

## Print / reports

From warrant detail, use the toolbar **reports / report-lister** for warrant packages your agency configured. Search-grid print may also be available from Search for filtered lists.

Court-side warrant documents (for example arrest warrant prints from a court violation) may be generated from **Court**, not only from WAR — use the path your training specifies.

## Attachments

Use the **Attachments** tab for supporting files allowed by policy (scans of paper warrants, returns, and related documents).

## History

The **History** tab shows an audit-style timeline of changes and events on the warrant. Use it when reconstructing who updated status, added service, or when court sync changed the record.

## Tips

- Re-print after status or service changes so paper matches the system.
- Attach the signed return when your agency keeps electronic copies.
- Warrant Map and other support-only tools are not part of everyday officer navigation.
