# Close Patrol

![Close Patrol search](images/rms-close-patrol-search.png)

Customer guides for the **Close Patrol (CLO)** module — short records for property checks, vacation watches, and similar close-patrol activity.

## What Close Patrol is

A **close patrol** is a focused RMS record for a location (and often a contact person) that officers check on a schedule or request. It is lighter than a full [Incident](../incidents/README.md) and separate from [Notepad](../notepad/README.md) field contacts.

| Use Close Patrol when… | Prefer another module when… |
|------------------------|-----------------------------|
| A residence or business needs recurring checks | You need a full offense/arrest report → **Incidents** |
| You are logging check activity over time | You are documenting a street contact → **Notepad** |

Nav abbreviation is **CLO** (module key may show as **CPT**).

## Who this guide is for

- Patrol and community officers who create or update close patrols
- Records staff who search and print close-patrol files
- Supervisors reviewing logs and attachments
