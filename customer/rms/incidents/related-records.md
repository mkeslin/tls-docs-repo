# Related records

How incidents connect to CAD, citations, warrants, and jail.

## Manage Associated Records

From incident detail, **Manage Associated Records** (record groups) links related RMS work such as:

| Related record | Typical relationship |
|----------------|----------------------|
| **CAD call** | Also shown as Associated Calls on General — dispatch origin |
| **Citation** | Citation created or linked to the incident |
| **Warrant** | Warrant associated to the case |
| **Notepad** | Related notepad / informational record |

Open the grouper to associate or navigate to linked records. Keep associations accurate so search and packets stay coherent.

## CAD

- Create or link the incident from the call sheet when the event was dispatched — see [Add an incident](add.md).
- Review associated calls on [General](general.md) for times and call numbers.

## Citations

Citations can create or link an incident through record association. Officer citation workflow is documented under [Citations](../citations/README.md); use associations when one event needs both a ticket and a full incident report.

## Jail

Suspect **Booked in Jail** on the incident is a case indicator. Booking, housing, and release are done in [Jail](../../jail/README.md). Jail Intake may reference incident arrestee information during booking.

## Court

Court cases usually come from **citations** or other charging paths, not a first-class Incidents tab. See [Citation to court](../citations/citation-to-court.md) and [Court](../../court/README.md).
