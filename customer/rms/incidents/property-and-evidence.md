# Property and evidence

Property on the incident and links to Evidence room records.

## Property tab

Open **Property** to record property involved in the incident (stolen, damaged, recovered, and related categories per your codes). Link **master property** when your agency uses property masters.

Complete property lines that IBRS or your policy requires for the offenses on the case.

## Evidence tab

Open **Evidence** for evidence numbers / items associated to the incident. Day-to-day evidence room inventory, custody, and audit live primarily in the **Evidence (EVI)** module; the incident tab connects the case to those items.

| Where | Use |
|-------|-----|
| **Incident → Evidence** | Case-linked evidence references |
| **Evidence module** | Inventory, chain of custody, audits |

## Tips

- Add property/evidence before final approval when the case depends on it for clearance or IBRS.
- Do not treat the incident Property tab as a full substitute for Evidence module procedures.
- Master property duplicates cause the same problems as duplicate people — search first.
