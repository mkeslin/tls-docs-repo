# Print, attachments, history, and IBRS

Reports, files, audit history, and state reporting readiness.

## Print / reports

From incident detail, use the toolbar **reports / report-lister** (screen reports for Incident Details) to print the packages your agency configured (full report, supplements, and related prints).

There is typically **no** Incidents drawer **Reports** item — printing is from the open incident (and sometimes search exports when offered).

## Attachments

Use the **Attachments** tab for supporting files allowed by policy (photos, scans, external documents). Attachments remain with the incident for later review.

## History

The **History** tab shows audit-style events: workflow changes, field history, and often **IBRS** transmission-related timeline entries when your agency uses them.

Use History when reconstructing who approved a version or when a batch was sent.

## IBRS (state reporting)

| Step | Where |
|------|--------|
| Mark offenses **Report to IBRS** | Offenses tab |
| **Validate** incident | Detail toolbar |
| Build / send IBRS batches | **Import/Export → IBRS** (authorized roles) |
| Review past activity | History / IBRS history views |

UI language is primarily **IBRS**; some permission names still say NIBRS. Follow your state and agency submission calendar.

## Analytics

Incident charts may appear under **Analytics → Incidents** for licensed users. Analytics does not replace the incident report or IBRS export.
