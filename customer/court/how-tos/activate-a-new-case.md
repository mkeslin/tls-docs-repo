# Activate a new case

![New case ready for activation](../images/court-violation-detail-new.png)

## Goal

Review a newly created court violation and activate it into **Pre-plea** with a first appearance date so it can appear on the docket.

## Prerequisites

- Court Violations access with modify rights
- Case in **New** (often from citation → court or New Case Review)
- Correct **court** agency selected in the header

## Steps

1. Open **Court Violations**.
2. Open **Work queues** → **New case review**, or search for the violation number.
3. Open the case.
4. Confirm defendant name, offense, and citation link.
5. Set or confirm the first **appearance** date (and time if your court uses it).
6. Choose **Activate** (or the equivalent enabled action) to move the case to **Pre-plea**.
7. Confirm the case left New Case Review and appears on **Calendar** for the appearance date.

If the case should not proceed in your court, use **Transfer** or **Void** per agency policy instead of activating.

## Expected result

- Procedural state is **Pre-plea** (or your court’s active intake state).
- Appearance date is set.
- Case is available for plea, scheduling, and payment actions as rules allow.
