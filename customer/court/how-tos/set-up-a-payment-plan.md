# Set up a payment plan

![Court case with payment plan](../images/court-payment-plan.png)

## Goal

Create an installment payment plan on a court violation and confirm it is active on the case.

## Prerequisites

- Case eligible for a plan (often **Convicted** with a balance; follow local policy)
- Court authorization for installments
- Modify rights; fee schedule awareness for time-payment fees if your court assesses them

## Steps

1. Open the court violation.
2. Confirm the balance and that judgment / disposition allows a plan.
3. Choose **Create payment plan**.
4. Enter the schedule (frequency, installment amounts, start date) and any required fees.
5. Save.
6. Confirm the case shows an active payment plan (header / search indicators / View payment plan).

## Missed payments later

When an installment is missed, work the **missed payment / compliance** queues and follow your court’s notice and show-cause process — see [Payment plans](../payment-plans.md).

## Expected result

- Active payment plan linked to the case.
- Installments visible for follow-up and queues.
